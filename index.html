Research Overview

Social networking sites (SNSs) have become a popular type of communication where groups of people virtually meet and interact with other people with similar interests. The use of SNS continues to dramatically increase when compare to the past. People spend so much time online. To understand how people interact with SNSs, collecting data is an important first step for analyzing usage behavior of SNSs.

We will ask for your help to cooperate in our research. Your information will be kept strictly confidential.
